About Gentle Cuts

Gentle Cuts was an MP3 blog created in 2010 by Albin Karlsson and Tobias Gerhardsson. It was used for sharing music which they liked and found inspiring, and was active for nearly a decade.

The Instagram account was updated regularly between July 27th and August 3rd, 2022. There hasn't been any activity anywhere since then.

The website's disappearance and domain expiration are currently unaddressed, leaving uncertainty about whether it was WordPress or the owners that took it down. Like many old and long-running projects, was it simply just forgotten, or abandoned?
